Information We Collect and How We Collect It

Information You Provide to Us.

  • Entrepôt collects information that you provide directly to us when you sign up and use the Entrepôt service including:
  • Information when you register or update the details of your account,
  • with other users, provide reviews or other comments, purchase services from entrepot,
  • request customer support, or otherwise communicate with us.

The types of information we may collect include:

  • your name,
  • email address,
  • telephone number,
  • postal address including city, state, and zip code,
  • photographs of posted items,
  • written descriptions of your posted items,
  • bids you make on items,
  • reviews or comments you make on the entrepot Service,
  • all messages with other users or with us sent through the entrepot messaging service or to entrepot directly,
  • any other information you choose to provide.

How We Use Your Information.

  • Verify your login information to allow you to access and use the Entrepôt Service, to personalize the entrepot Service,
  • and to optimize the location and type of offers presented to you when you use the entrepot Service;
  • Connect you to other users on the entrepot Service via friends lists when authorized by you;
  • Create and maintain a trusted and safer environment on the OfferUp Service, including through detection and prevention of fraud, unauthorized access, intrusion, and service interruption;
  • Conduct investigations and to respond to disputes between users, error resolution, and other similar customer support service;
  • participate, such as verifying your identity and address, and conduct checks against various publicly available databases;
  • Operate, protect, improve and optimize the Entrepôt Service, our business, and our users’ experience; Perform analytics, conduct research, and monitor and analyze trends, usage and activities in connection with the OfferUp Service;
  • Communicate with you about products, services, offers, promotions, rewards, and events offered by Entrepôt and others;
